Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 5, 2026

Last Updated: April 5, 2026

IronLog ("the App") is operated by Stefan Bocanegra ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Profile Information: Name, age, height, weight, body fat estimate, training experience, goals, and injury records you enter during onboarding or in settings.
  • Workout Data: Training programs, exercise logs, set/rep/weight data, RPE ratings, session notes, and workout duration.
  • Progress Photos: Photos you optionally attach to workout sessions, stored on your device and synced via iCloud if enabled.
  • Coach Conversations: Messages you send to the AI coach and the responses generated.
  • Community Content: Documents, research, or programs you voluntarily upload to the community library (requires Sign in with Apple).

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Apple ID (Community Only): If you sign in with Apple to use community features, we receive a unique identifier and optionally your name. We never receive your email address unless you choose to share it.
  • Usage Analytics: We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or telemetry. The App does not include any third-party analytics SDKs.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your location, contacts, calendar, microphone audio, health data from HealthKit, or browsing history.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers or participate in ad networks.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Core App Functionality: All workout data, profile information, and progress photos are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data powers your training log, statistics, progression tracking, and AI coaching context.
  • iCloud Sync: If you have iCloud enabled, your data syncs across your Apple devices via CloudKit. Apple manages this sync; we do not have access to your iCloud data.
  • AI Coaching: When you use the AI coach, your profile context and recent workout data are sent to the AI provider you configure (e.g., a local server, OpenAI, or another provider). We do not operate these AI services. See Section 4 for details.
  • Community Library: Content you upload to the community library is stored on our Supabase-hosted database and is visible to other users. Your display name (from Sign in with Apple) appears as the author.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local-First Architecture: The vast majority of your data never leaves your device. Workout logs, training programs, stats, and coach conversations are stored in a local SwiftData database.
  • Encryption: All network communication uses HTTPS/TLS. API credentials you configure are stored in the iOS Keychain with device-only protection (not included in backups).
  • Community Data: Community documents, votes, and reports are stored on Supabase (hosted on AWS). Row-level security ensures users can only modify their own data.
  • No Server-Side Processing: We do not process, analyze, or mine your workout data on our servers. The community backend only stores content you explicitly upload.

4. Third-Party AI Providers

The AI coach feature requires you to configure an AI provider. This is entirely your choice:

  • Local Providers (e.g., Ollama): AI processing happens entirely on your device or local network. No data leaves your control.
  • Cloud Providers (e.g., OpenAI): When you configure a cloud AI provider, your profile context and workout data are sent to that provider's API to generate coaching responses. You provide your own API key. We are not a party to your relationship with that provider, and their privacy policy governs how they handle your data.
  • Clipboard Mode: You manually copy prompts and paste responses. No automated data transmission occurs.

We do not operate, control, or have access to any AI provider you configure. Your API keys are stored locally in the iOS Keychain and are never transmitted to us.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Community Content: Documents you upload to the community library are visible to other users by design.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

6. Your Rights and Controls

  • Export: You can export all your data as a JSON file from Settings > Export Data at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete all app data from Settings > Export Data > Reset All Data. This removes all local data, clears credentials, and signs you out of community services.
  • Community Content Removal: You can delete your own community uploads at any time.
  • Account Deletion: Since we use Sign in with Apple, you can revoke access from your Apple ID settings at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last Updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
